The Famous TikTok pasta

When out of the oven, the dish should look like this. Just add the cooked pasta and bon appetit!

This simple dish only needs a skillet, an oven, and a few ingredients

Once heated, all you need to do is throw in some cooked pasta and mix it all together for a tasty dinner!

  • 2 pt.cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 1 shallot, quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed/li>
  • 1/2 c extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 block feta cheese
  • 3 sprigs of thyme
  • 10 oz pasta
  1. Add the cherry tomatoes in the skillet
  2. Place the block of feta in the middle
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, etc.
